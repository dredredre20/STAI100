Position: Cloud Engineer
Company: RCBC
Location: Taguig, NCR
Pay: N/A


Job Summary:
Responsible for the overall planning, organizing, and execution of assigned IT infrastructure and systems in accordance 
with bank standards and MTPP compliance laws and regulations. This includes managing assigned IT operations and 
functions to meet customer requirements as well as the support and maintenance of existing applications and development of new technical solutions. 


How will you contribute?
- Acts as the lead for application system enhancements or mid to complex infrastructure projects and changes.
- Ensures the consistency and maintainability of existing applications by creating, maintaining, and enforcing standards/procedures for implementing technical solutions.
- Directs operations in executing production tasks according to a documented schedule that meets or exceeds customer expectations.
- Assigns workload to identify and balance the duties and responsibilities performed by each project team member.
- Ensures that project adheres to quality assurance standards and guidelines. 
- Reviews performance of existing application system in production and recommends enhancements/modifications for improved efficiency. 
- Ensures adherence to the bank’s methodology and standards on systems development, information security and audit. Recommends solutions to address audit findings.


What will make you successful?
- Graduate of any IT-related course
- Over 5 years of experience in systems analysis, design, and development, focusing on cloud and diverse computing environments.
- Proven experience managing AWS cloud platform with experience in Kubernetes and Container Technologies (EKS and Openshift)
- Proficiency in scripting and automation tools (e.g., Terraform, Python).
- Demonstrable skills in project management and familiarity with agile methodologies.
- Willing to work in Taguig
