Your Role And Responsibilities:
As an Infrastructure Specialist for the Azure Platform, you will manage and implement Azure-based 
infrastructure solutions according to established designs, ensuring scalability, reliability, and 
security. You will collaborate with IT teams to implement and integrate Azure infrastructure solutions based on existing designs.


Your Primary Responsibilities Will Include:
- Set up and Configure Azure Resources: Set up, configure, and manage Azure Virtual Machines, storage solutions, networking, and databases to effectively support business applications. This includes implementing and overseeing essential Azure resources such as Virtual Networks, Load Balancers, and Application Gateways.
- Monitor and Troubleshoot Azure Infrastructure: Continuously monitor Azure infrastructure, troubleshoot issues, and maintain high availability (HA) and disaster recovery (DR) configurations to ensure reliable performance.
- Apply Security Measures: Apply and enforce security measures to ensure Azure infrastructure complies with organizational policies and industry regulations.
- Optimize Azure Costs: Optimize Azure costs by right-sizing resources, developing cost-effective solutions, and monitoring usage patterns.
- Automate Processes: Use automation tools like Azure Automation, Terraform, and Ansible to automate and streamline processes.
