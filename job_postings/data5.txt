Position: Data Scientist
Company: Stratpoint Technologies
Location: Mandaluyong, NCR
Pay: N/A


The Role:

The Data Scientist plays a key role in supporting the Data Team’s vision of building a data 
ecosystem that delivers accurate, timely, and well-structured data to enable the cycle of data asset enrichment 
for broader enterprise impact. This role is both technical and strategic — contributing to the delivery of high-quality 
data and model solutions while also helping build data foundations through active involvement in data governance initiatives.

The Data Scientist will collaborate closely with both internal stakeholders 
(Product Leads, Machine Learning Engineers, and Software Developers) as well as external stakeholders'
(inc. other business units) as they enable use cases, advance operational efficiency and effectiveness, and champion data-driven culture.


Your Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement forecasting models, including time series and hierarchical forecasting frameworks (e.g., bottom-up, top-down, reconciliation methods)
- Apply statistical, machine learning, and deep learning techniques to solve real-world prediction problems
- Design and maintain automated data pipelines, including data ingestion, preprocessing, model training, and output generation
- Work with sparse, noisy, or incomplete datasets, ensuring robustness and reliability of models
- Perform data analysis and visualization, including the creation of clear and insightful forecasting graphs and dashboards
- Integrate data from open-source datasets and external data vendors
- Conduct basic web scraping and/or develop synthetic data generation pipelines where data is limited
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate business requirements into scalable AI solutions


About You:
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Physics, Math, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related quantitative field
- Proven experience in time series forecasting and statistical modeling
- Solid understanding of: Machine Learning (e.g., regression, tree-based models, ensemble methods); Deep Learning (e.g., RNNs, LSTMs, Transformers for time series); Statistical methods (e.g., inference, probabilistic modeling)
- Experience working with hierarchical or multi-level datasets
- Proficiency in Python, common data science libraries and tools such as: NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, PyTorch/TensorFlow, Nixtla; Data visualization frameworks (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly); Fast API, Streamlit, Gradio; Docker, Git; AWS (S3, RDS, Redshift); SQL; Marimo (or similar reproducible notebooks); Databricks or similar platforms
- Experience building end-to-end data workflows, including ingestion, transformation, modeling, and output
- Strong skills in data visualization and graphical analysis
- Effective communication skills, and able to clearly document work (pipelines, flowcharts, ERDs, BRDs)


Nice to have:
- Experience in healthcare or financial data domains, including cost optimization use cases
- Experience with data engineering concepts (ETL pipelines, data warehousing), and can integrate open-source and external data
- Experience with web scraping tools (e.g., BeautifulSoup, Scrapy) or synthetic data generation techniques
- Experience with distributed data systems and scalable computing
- Familiarity with sparse data modeling techniques and uncertainty quantification
- Exposure to cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Knowledge of MLOps practices (model deployment, monitoring, CI/CD for ML systems)
- Familiarity with data governance, including frameworks for data quality checks, lineage, security and compliance
- Familiarity with experiment tracking and model versioning tools
- Understanding of optimization techniques and numerical methods
