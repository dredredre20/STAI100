Position: Cloud Engineer 
Company: WPH Digital PH
Location: Alabang
Pay: Php 25,000 - 50,000

Job Description:
Under the administrative supervision of the Technology team, this position is responsible for the deployment, 
operation, and maintenance of a cloud‑based application infrastructure.

The role involves deploying, automating, maintaining, troubleshooting, and continuously improving systems to ensure smooth 
and reliable backend operations. It requires strong hands‑on technical expertise, a proactive mindset, and a practical, can‑do 
approach to environment automation, system management, and continuous improvement.


Job Duties:
- Deploy, automate, maintain, and manage AWS cloud‑based production systems to ensure availability, performance, scalability, and security.
- Handle build, release, and configuration management of production systems.
- Perform pre‑production acceptance testing to help ensure the quality of products and services.
- Troubleshoot and resolve system issues across platform and application domains.
- Recommend architecture enhancements and process improvements.
- Ensure critical system security using best‑in‑class cloud security solutions.
- Evaluate new technologies and third‑party vendor products.


Skills and Requirements:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or an equivalent technical discipline.
- At least 2 years of hands‑on experience using a broad range of AWS services (e.g., EC2, RDS, ELB, EBS, S3, VPC, Glacier, IAM, CloudWatch, KMS), with a strong focus on cloud security best practices.
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect (Associate) is preferred.
- Familiarity with infrastructure‑as‑code tools such as CloudFormation and Terraform.
- Experience working as a DevOps Engineer in a 24x7 AWS production environment, including automation using configuration management tools.
- Strong scripting and automation skills.
- Solid experience in both Windows and Linux system administration.
- Hands‑on experience with system monitoring tools.
- Strong problem‑solving skills with the ability to diagnose and resolve complex infrastructure and application deployment issues.
- Experience with other cloud platforms such as Azure or GCP is a plus.
